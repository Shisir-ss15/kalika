About Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School

Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School (KMGSS), established in 2018 B.S. in Butwal, Nepal, is recognized as the largest community school in the country, serving over 8,000 students from Early Childhood Development (ECD) to Grade 12.

It is known for its high academic standards, ISA certification, and comprehensive educational programs.

Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School is situated at Kalikanagar, Butwal-10, Rupandehi, Nepal. With the current student population of 8,000, the school offers a wide range of courses and activities to cater to the diverse interests and needs of its students.

The school is committed to providing quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment for all its students.
